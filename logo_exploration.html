Brand Identity

Ember Sports
Logo Exploration

A comprehensive study of visual identity concepts for a modern sports venue and community platform.

Brand Context

Understanding the Names

Each brand name carries distinct meaning and cultural resonance. The visual identity should honor these layers while creating a cohesive, memorable mark.

Primary Brand

Ember
合拍社

Ember evokes fire, spark, and energy — the glowing heart of flame. 合拍社 suggests harmony, being in sync, and community gathering.

Origin Story

Off the Zone
新加坡起源

Started as a badminton academy in Singapore, evolved into venue operations. Represents breaking boundaries and peak performance.

Parent Company

聚芯
Jù Xīn

聚 means to gather/assemble; 芯 means core/heart. Represents bringing people together at the heart of community.

Design Direction

Modern Minimalist
现代极简

Clean lines, geometric precision, simple typography. Inspired by Nike, Airbnb — bold yet refined, memorable yet versatile.

Logo Concepts

Six Visual Directions

Each concept explores a different facet of the brand story — from the spark of energy to the harmony of connection, from the court itself to the rally of play.
